The Hampden County Mesh Network is an evolving community project focused on infrastructure, experimentation, monitoring, and regional growth.

ROADMAP OVERVIEW

This roadmap outlines the current priorities, active projects, and future goals of the Hampden County Mesh Network.

As a community-driven initiative, priorities may evolve as infrastructure expands and new opportunities emerge.

COMPLETED MILESTONES

Website Launch

Public website established and available for project information and documentation.

Observer Deployment

Network observation infrastructure deployed and operational.

MQTT Services

Mosquitto MQTT broker deployed, operational, and supporting ongoing infrastructure testing.

Core Infrastructure

MeshCore-Hub operational with logging, analysis, and development capabilities.
