WHY GUIDES MATTER

Hampden County Mesh is built around shared local knowledge. A useful communications network is not just hardware on a hill or a node in a window. It depends on people knowing how to test, document, repair, explain, and teach the system to others.

These guides are meant to help neighbors, operators, educators, makers, and community groups learn practical skills without needing permission from a company, platform, or institution to begin.

More guides will be added as the project grows, including node setup, field testing, mapping, community events, and operator notes.
