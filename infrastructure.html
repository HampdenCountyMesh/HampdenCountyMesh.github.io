NETWORK STANDARDS

Hampden County Mesh currently focuses on MeshCore as its primary mesh networking platform.

Infrastructure is deployed using United States frequency allocations and standard MeshCore configuration parameters.

Operators are encouraged to verify regional settings, frequency plans, and network configuration before deployment.

Additional standards and deployment guidance will be published as the network expands.

LONG-TERM GOALS

Build a resilient, community-owned communications network throughout Hampden County and surrounding regions.

Encourage technical education, radio experimentation, and public participation in mesh networking.

Promote interoperability and cooperation with neighboring communities and communications projects throughout New England.
