Coverage Notes

Coverage depends on terrain, height, antennas, and placement.

Hampden County has hills, valleys, dense neighborhoods, wooded areas, river corridors, older buildings, campuses, parks, and rural edges. Small radio devices can behave very differently from one place to another.

A node near a window, outside, higher up, or connected to a better antenna may hear more than the same node sitting deeper inside a building or vehicle.

The dedicated map address is map.hampdencountymesh.org. For now, it points here. Later, it may point to a live or near-live map when that system is ready.

Local Terrain
Small radio devices behave differently depending on terrain, buildings, antennas, and height.

Radio changes from place to place.

A short move can matter. A parking lot, hill, window, field, vehicle, or wooded trail may all produce different results.

Testing locally helps people understand what works here instead of relying only on guesses, spec sheets, or results from other regions.
