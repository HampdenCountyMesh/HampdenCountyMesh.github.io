LICENSE-FREE RADIO

FRS

Family Radio Service

  • No FCC license required
  • 22 channels
  • Excellent for families and events
  • Shared channel plan with GMRS

MURS

Multi-Use Radio Service

  • No FCC license required
  • 5 VHF channels
  • Often performs well in wooded areas
  • External antennas permitted
151.820 MHz
151.880 MHz
151.940 MHz
154.570 MHz
154.600 MHz
GMRS

General Mobile Radio Service

  • FCC license required
  • No exam required
  • Repeaters allowed
  • Excellent local-area communications

CB RADIO

Citizens Band Radio

  • No FCC license required
  • 40 channels
  • Channel 19 highway calling
  • Channel 9 emergency monitoring
AMATEUR RADIO

Ham Radio

  • FCC license required
  • Technician Class is the entry license
  • Local and worldwide communications
146.520 MHz — National 2m Calling
446.000 MHz — National 70cm Calling
