# Asset Credits and Usage

This document covers licensing, attribution, privacy, and reuse of images, icons, screenshots, and other media in the Hampden County Mesh repository.

## Licensing

Unless otherwise stated, the repository’s MIT License applies to source code, templates, stylesheets, scripts, configuration, and documentation.

It does not automatically grant reuse rights for:

- Hampden County Mesh logos or branding
- Photographs
- Social preview images
- Screenshots
- Contributor-provided media
- Third-party logos, icons, or trademarks

Hampden County Mesh photographs and branded media are not offered under the MIT License unless a separate license is stated.

Contact Hampden County Mesh before reusing an image when permission is unclear.

## Photographs and Contributor Media

Unless otherwise noted, site photographs were taken by the Hampden County Mesh maintainer or provided with permission for HCM use.

Contributor-provided media should only be published when permission is clear. Keep a record of:

- Who supplied the media
- What use was approved
- Whether attribution was requested
- Whether editing was allowed
- Whether reuse outside HCM was approved

Permission to use an image on the HCM website does not automatically permit unrelated reuse.

## Privacy Review

Before publishing an image, screenshot, or map, check for:

- Private addresses, coordinates, or installation locations
- Credentials, keys, tokens, or administrative information
- Private messages or account details
- License plates, documents, badges, or identifying information
- People who did not agree to be shown
- GPS, EXIF, or other hidden metadata

Crop, redact, blur, or replace material when necessary. Remove location and identifying metadata from public photographs when practical.

See the [Sharing Safely](https://hampdencountymesh.org/guides/sharing-safely.html) guide for broader public-sharing guidance.

## File Names and Organization

Use descriptive filenames that do not expose private information.

Examples:

- `mt-tom-towers-01.jpg`
- `wio-tracker-field-test-01.jpg`
- `node-demo-table-01.jpg`
- `solar-node-example-01.jpg`

Do not place personal names, private addresses, coordinates, credentials, or temporary joke names in filenames.

Organize media by purpose where practical:

- `assets/images/hero/`
- `assets/images/community/`
- `assets/images/coverage/`
- `assets/images/guides/`
- `assets/images/equipment/`
- `assets/images/social/`
- `assets/icons/`

## Maps and Screenshots

Map screenshots and observed-activity images may be incomplete and should not imply that:

- Every regional node is shown
- Every displayed node is currently reachable
- Every node or packet belongs to Hampden County Mesh
- Displayed locations establish ownership of infrastructure

Generalize or omit sensitive locations where appropriate.

See `docs/data-access-and-observed-activity.md` for detailed map and observed-activity guidance.
