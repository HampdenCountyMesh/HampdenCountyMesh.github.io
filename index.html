Why mesh communications?

Mesh communications let small radios pass short messages from one nearby node to another. A message can move across an area without depending on cell service, Wi-Fi, or internet access.

Platforms like MeshCore and Meshtastic make this easier to explore with affordable hardware, phone apps, and low-power radio. They give local people a practical way to learn, test coverage, share messages, and build more resilient communications from the ground up.
