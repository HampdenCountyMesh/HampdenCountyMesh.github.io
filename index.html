WHAT THIS IS

Mesh radio and radio education in Western Massachusetts

Hampden County Mesh is a local community for people interested in MeshCore, Meshtastic, beginner-friendly radio education, and practical off-grid communications.

We focus on helping more people get on the mesh, understand the basics, and use radio to communicate in Western Massachusetts.

TERRAIN MATTERS
Mount Tom radio towers overlooking Hampden County.

Terrain, height, buildings, and trees all matter.

Hampden County has hills, valleys, dense neighborhoods, wooded areas, old mill buildings, open farmland, and high spots like Mount Tom. All of that changes how small radios behave.

Mesh radio is hands-on. A better antenna, a better location, or a little more height can make a real difference.
