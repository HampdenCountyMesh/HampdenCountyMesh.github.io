Why mesh communications?

Mesh radios can pass short messages directly between nearby devices. Messages may also travel through other nodes, extending communication across a wider area without relying on cell service or normal internet access.

MeshCore and Meshtastic make it possible to explore this with small, low-power hardware. Hampden County Mesh is a place to learn how it works and see what is practical in our region.
