Meshtastic basics

MeshCore values here follow the broader Massachusetts pattern of 910.525 MHz, 62.5 kHz bandwidth, SF7, CR5, US region, and 2-byte path hashes for repeaters. Normal Meshtastic users should generally stay on LongFast/default and CLIENT unless they have a real fixed infrastructure use case.

Local terrain

Height, terrain, buildings, and trees all matter.

Hampden County has hills, valleys, dense neighborhoods, wooded areas, river corridors, old mill buildings, rural edges, and high spots. Mesh coverage changes across all of it.

Mt. Tom is used here as a local visual reference, not as a claim that HCM operates any particular mountaintop, tower, repeater, or site.
