Why mesh radio?

Mesh radio lets small devices pass short messages from one nearby node to another. A message can move across an area without depending on cell service, Wi-Fi, or normal internet access.

MeshCore and Meshtastic make this easier to explore with affordable hardware, phone apps, and low-power radio. Around Hampden County, it is a practical way to learn, try local routes, send short messages, and understand what small radios can do here.
