Hampden County Mesh

A local community for learning, testing, and building with MeshCore and Meshtastic across Hampden County, Western Massachusetts, and nearby New England.

Start Here View Live Map

Local radio, built around our region.

Mesh radios can pass short messages directly between nearby devices. When other nodes are available, messages may travel farther across the network without relying entirely on cell service or internet access.

These systems can be useful for everyday communication, field testing, community events, and added resilience during outages or disruptions. Hampden County Mesh is a place to learn what works across the hills, valleys, cities, and towns of Western Massachusetts.

New to mesh radio? Learn what you need and how to get started locally. Already have a device? Apply the recommended settings used around the region. Looking for local activity? Open the live map and explore recently observed radio traffic. Want to improve local coverage? See how terrain, height, placement, and fixed nodes affect radio paths.

MeshCore and Meshtastic

Hampden County Mesh supports both open-source radio projects, with MeshCore as the main focus for shared regional infrastructure.

MeshCore

MeshCore is the primary focus for local messaging, repeaters, observers, and connections with neighboring mesh communities.

Meshtastic

HCM also supports Meshtastic for portable communication, telemetry, experimentation, and people who already use Meshtastic devices.
