WHAT THIS MEANS

Site-maintained systems online

This site currently maintains a small amount of supporting infrastructure, including an observer, a local hub, and tools for logging, checking, and understanding mesh activity.

Observed activity tools in development

Public activity views, maps, dashboards, and automated stats are planned, but they should not be treated as complete or authoritative yet.

Local community building

The larger goal is to help local operators, beginners, radio users, makers, and nearby communities find each other and compare notes.

New participants welcome

You do not need to be an expert, run infrastructure, or fill out formal reports to participate. Questions and casual notes are welcome.

SITE-MAINTAINED SYSTEMS

One contribution to a wider ecosystem.

Hampden County Mesh currently maintains a MeshCore Room Observer, a Linux-based MeshCore-Hub, and local tools for logging, checking, and documenting observed activity.

These systems are useful, but they are not the whole local mesh ecosystem. Other operators, nodes, observers, and repeaters may exist independently and may not be affiliated with this site.

Part of the goal is to connect with those people, share notes, and help build a clearer picture of local mesh and radio activity together.

A window into local activity.

Future live activity tools should help people see nearby mesh activity, understand what may be reachable, and compare notes with others interested in local radio and mesh networking.

Activity views may include information heard by site-maintained systems or other public sources where available. They should be treated as a partial view, not a complete picture of everyone using mesh in Hampden County.

The purpose is to make local activity easier to understand and help people connect around practical communications.

STATUS DEFINITIONS

Online

A site-maintained system exists, is documented, and is currently considered usable for community and documentation work.

Development

A feature or service is being built, tested, or planned, but should not be treated as complete, reliable, or authoritative yet.

Building

The community is still growing through learning, casual signal checks, documentation, local participation, and connecting people.

Unknown

A system, node, or coverage area has not been recently verified. Unknown does not always mean offline; it means more observation or local knowledge is needed.

IMPORTANT LIMITS

Hampden County Mesh is a community communications and learning effort. It is not an emergency service, not a replacement for 911, not an official public safety system, and not a guarantee of coverage during an outage.

This site documents site-maintained systems, shares public guides, welcomes community notes, and helps people connect around the broader local mesh and radio ecosystem.

The community becomes more useful as more people learn, ask questions, compare setups, host nodes, share notes, and help others get started.
